About Me

I am the Program Director at StudentRND, which means I spend my days working to improve tech education.

I dropped out of college (but I don't generally recommend it).

In the past, I founded Framebase, a Y Combinator-backed startup which is sadly now defunct. Before that, I worked for several small startups, and as a research intern at Microsoft Research.
